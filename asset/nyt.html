Full-Stack Developer With 8 Years of Experience Seeks Next Challenge
Builder of HR systems, POS platforms, and property portals says the best code is code that solves real problems for real people.
By The Angga Times Staff Reporter
Tangerang, Monday

Angga Fartiteur Setiawan, a full-stack web developer based in Tangerang, has spent the better part of a decade crafting digital tools that sit at the intersection of functionality and visual design — a discipline he describes as both an engineering challenge and an artistic pursuit.

Mr. Setiawan, who began his career building point-of-sale software in 2018, has since expanded his repertoire to include human resource information systems, recruitment portals, attendance management applications, and company profile websites for the property sector.

"I have a passion for design," Mr. Setiawan said in a recent interview. "Any developer can make something work. I want to make something that people actually enjoy using."

"Fast-forward to today, and I've had the privilege of building software for a property company, a start-up, and a finance consulting firm."
— Angga Fartiteur Setiawan, Developer

His most recent project, a Progressive Web Application for employee attendance management, launched in 2025 and is available on both Android and iOS — a technical feat that demonstrates his growing fluency in mobile-first development.

Summary of Qualifications

Mr. Setiawan is a full-stack web developer with demonstrated expertise across the complete software development lifecycle. His work spans backend server architecture, relational database design, front-end interface engineering, and mobile-first progressive web application development.

He has delivered solutions for the property, retail, human resources, and startup sectors, with a consistent emphasis on combining technical rigour with considered visual design.

"I'm a full-stack web developer with a passion for design, capable of crafting visually stunning websites that seamlessly combine functionality and aesthetics."

What Sets This Developer Apart

Unlike many developers who specialise narrowly in either front-end presentation or back-end architecture, Mr. Setiawan's portfolio demonstrates genuine fluency across both disciplines, with the design sensibility of someone who has spent equal time studying what a site looks like as how it functions.

The breadth of his project history — spanning POS systems, HR platforms, property websites, and attendance applications — further indicates an adaptability that is uncommon at this level of technical proficiency.

#WKTK: Where Anime Aesthetics Meet Indonesian Entrepreneurship

The fashion label #WKTK — available through Tokopedia at the handle wakuteka — is not a side project so much as a second vocation. Mr. Setiawan designs clothing and accessories that pay homage to iconic anime characters and infuse traditional Japanese visual aesthetics into wearable form.

The Full-Time Traveler

Perhaps the most striking detail in Mr. Setiawan's self-description is the final line: I'm also a full-time traveler. The laptop, it appears, is not anchored to any single desk. The combination of remote-compatible development work, event photography, and an e-commerce fashion brand provides the infrastructure for a life lived across geographies.

It is an existence that his website, in its understated way, presents as entirely natural — the logical conclusion of building tools that work from anywhere, for anyone.
